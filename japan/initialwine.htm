Initial Wine

When I found this wine label, I was confused. Because I could not understand what "omaine" means. But soon I found a very big capital D, which "omaine" follows. It was very hard for me to find the D.

I wondered why such a big D was designed on this label, and I gotthe reason.

I think the designer of this wine label must be an enthusiastic fan of Chuunuchi DRAGONS, Japanese professional baseball team in Nagoya.
